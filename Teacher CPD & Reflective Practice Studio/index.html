About This Application

Teacher CPD & Reflective Practice Studio

Mateen Yousuf

Teacher · School Education Department Kashmir

This application was developed to empower teachers across Kashmir and India with a comprehensive, offline-first professional development tool — fully aligned with NEP 2020 and NCF 2023 frameworks.

The app requires no internet connection, no login, and no backend server — making it accessible to teachers in remote areas with limited connectivity.
